Welcome to the Project RISHI Village Map!

Project RISHI is a non-profit organization, whose mission is to promote the sustainable development and growth of rural Indian communities.

To get started, click on one of the wards on the left to explore the regions where projects are based. Project initiatives and landmarks can be filtered on the right.
